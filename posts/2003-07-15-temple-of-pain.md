---
title: Temple of Pain
tags: poetry
edited: 03/22/2004
location: Italy
---

    A gym
    is a Temple of Pain
    to which
    the votaries of Will retire
    and heap their bodies
    on pyres of agony
    burning away
    the minutes and the hours
    of their devotion
    until that exhaustion
    which is their spirit's joy
    is returned.
