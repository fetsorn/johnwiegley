---
title: This pen
tags: poetry
edited: 03/22/2004
location: Spain
---

| How powerful,
| this pen in my hand;
| capable of reshaping the world.
|
| By itself,
| lonely and impotent.
|
| But if the will of an intent mind
| wields it with intention,
| its sharp point
| begins to shine into the future,
| like a laser pointing out
| the form of tomorrow.
