---
title: Falling rain
tags: poetry
location: 27342 Via Burgos, Mission Viejo, CA
---

| the drops of the rain
| fall steadily down;
| the grey clouds
| the muted breeze
| the scents
| of a sodden earth.
| the water soaks
| into the ground's
| own marrow.
| the world is slowly
| being dissolved
| by a cleansing sky.
