---
title: A maple leaf
tags: poetry
edited: 03/22/2004
location: Italy
---

| A maple leaf
| is a webbed, green hand
| that waves at the breeze.
| The trunk of its arm,
| roughened by years,
| ending in a shoulder of hill.
| Where are the feet of such a man?
| if not the pebbly toes
| still cooling in a nearby stream.
