---
title: A cherished sound
tags: poetry
edited: 08/20/2004
location: 380 Esplanade #211
---

| The lilt of your gentle voice,
| its dulcet tones,
| its warm, mellifluous sound...
| to a man's ears, what balm,
| what anodyne peace;
| I linger there in memory
| until the pain of absence
| grows profound.
